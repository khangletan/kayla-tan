Objective and Data Description¶

United State is one of the countries where people consume alcohol on daily basis. As Business Analysts, we want to perform a detailed analysis on this topic to understand the trend of alcohol consumption in our home State - Iowa.

Our group has collected the data from Iowa Government Website (https://data.iowa.gov/Sales-Distribution/Iowa-Liquor-Sales/m3tr-qhgy/data). We did not have any challenges in downloading the dataset as it is publicly available. The dataset contains date, store number, store name, locations, county, liquor category, vendor information, item description, pack, bottle volume (ml), bottle sold, volume sold (liters and gallons), cost, sales, etc.

Analysis we would like to do¶

• Visualize the store location across Iowa using Geopandas.

• What is the trend of liquor sales across various year?

• What is the trend of liquor sales across months for different categories and years?

• What is the most preferred liquor type for different years?

• Identify the top 10 and bottom 10 stores or vendors based on sales.

• What is the most significant variable that influences the liquor sales of a store in Iowa?

def Liquor_Category(Category_Name):
    
    if 'Whisk' in str(Category_Name):
        return 'Whiskey'
    elif "Vodka" in str(Category_Name):
        return 'Vodka'
    elif "Rum" in str(Category_Name):
        return 'Rum'
    elif "Tequila" in str(Category_Name):
        return 'Tequila'
    elif "Gin" in str(Category_Name):
        return 'Gin'
    else:
        return 'Others'
    
liquorSales["Category of Liquor"] = liquorSales.Category_Name.apply(lambda x:Liquor_Category(x))
